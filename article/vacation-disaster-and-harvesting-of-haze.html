For our 8 year anniversary we decided to go to Las Vegas and celebrate! I was sure I was prepared, as the automatic watering system was working great! I forgot one little thing however. I forgot to turn on the air conditioner in my office. Since the grow shares space with a few servers, here in Arizona it gets hot quickly, even during the winter. Which means we came home to a sweltering 90f degree office.

This is what we were greeted by:

Haze was very wilted and yellow, and sickly looking. The rest were only marginally better. We put them on a regime of water and they seem to have mostly recovered.
