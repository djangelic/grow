For the past 6 months, we have been on a quest to find a specific strain for a family member of ours who wanted to propagate this strain at their farm. We have looked and looked to no avail, at least until last night. We placed an order for 20 Land Race seeds, specifically the strain Swazi Gold.

The provider is Pheno Paradise. I have not ordered seeds from them before, but I am excited to see what we get! I was pointed to them via a Reddit post, so super excited to see what we get.

Their description for this strain reads as follows:

Landrace Sativa, Beautiful structure and easy to grow. Ready early for a pure sativa middle October with some rare phenos hitting end of October. Great sativa high.

Leafly's say's the following:

Swazi Gold is a sativa landrace strain from Africa known for its sweet, citrus flavor and fast-acting effects. Designed to withstand the harsh conditions of its mountainous homeland, Swazi Gold grows with ease and resilience, although growers will have to wait anywhere from 55 to 85 days for plants to finish flowering.
